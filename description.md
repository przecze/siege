Siege!! - craft your units with pattern matching to overcome the opponent castle


In this game they are two castles at the two ends of 1D battlefield. The players can spawn units from they castles that start advancing towards the enemy and fight the opponent units they encounter. Player who spawns more units faster will be able to make them reach the opposite end of the battlefield and deal damage to the enemy castle. Crafting the units happens separately on a 2D grid with blocks representing 4 resources. To craft a unit, player has to move the blocks fast to form a pattern - the complexity of the pattern depends on the unit, starting with very simple, 2-block pattern for an infantry and ending with very-complicated grid for spawning a dragon.
